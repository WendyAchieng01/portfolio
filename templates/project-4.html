Autospares Website - Your One-Stop Auto Parts Shop

A dynamic e-commerce platform specializing in automotive parts and accessories. Features advanced search by vehicle make/model/year, detailed product specifications with compatibility information, secure checkout process, and inventory management system that ensures accurate stock levels and timely delivery.

Technology: Django, PostgreSQL, Paystack API

Duration: 3 months

Features: Vehicle search, Shopping cart, Order tracking

This comprehensive automotive e-commerce solution transforms the car parts shopping experience with intelligent search functionality and detailed product information. Customers can easily find compatible parts using their vehicle's VIN, make, model, or year. The platform features high-quality product images, installation guides, and customer reviews to aid purchasing decisions. The backend includes a sophisticated inventory management system that syncs with suppliers, automated reorder alerts, and comprehensive sales analytics. Additional features include wish lists, comparison tools, warranty tracking, and a loyalty rewards program. The mobile-responsive design ensures customers can shop conveniently from any device, while multiple payment gateways and shipping options provide flexibility and convenience.
